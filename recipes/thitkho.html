Thịt Kho Hột Vịt

Description

Thịt kho hột vịt is a beloved Vietnamese dish that combines tender pork belly and hard-boiled eggs simmered in a rich, caramelized sauce. This comforting dish is often served with steamed rice, making it a staple in many households. The balance of savory, sweet, and slightly salty flavors creates a delightful harmony that warms the heart.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Coconut water
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce

Instructions

  1. Cut the pork belly into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a pot, caramelize sugar until golden brown.
  3. Add pork and cook until browned.
  4. Add coconut water and fish sauce; simmer until tender.
  5. Add hard-boiled eggs and cook for an additional 10 minutes.

*Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy the rich flavors of this traditional Vietnamese dish.
