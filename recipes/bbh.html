Bún Bò Huế

Description

Bún bò Huế is the soul of Central Vietnam in a bowl — a symphony of spice, citrus, and savory depth. Born in the ancient capital of Huế, it carries whispers of royal kitchens and street-side stalls alike. With its fragrant lemongrass broth, crimson chili oil, and tender meats atop thick rice noodles, each bite tells a story of heat, heritage, and heart.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank
  • Rice noodles
  • Herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Lime
  • Chili paste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the broth with beef shank and spices.
  2. Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Assemble the soup with noodles, broth, herbs, and lime.
  4. Add chili paste to taste.
